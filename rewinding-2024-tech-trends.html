Rewinding the Clock: How Watchmaker Marketing Capitalized on 2024 Tech & Trends

Watchmaker Marketing · February 6, 2025 · 3 min read

As we kick off 2025, we're taking a moment to reflect on an incredible 2024. This past year, we embraced new trends, tools, and strategies to help local businesses succeed in an ever-changing marketing landscape. From tapping into NIL endorsements to harnessing the power of streaming TV and AI, our efforts have delivered real results for our clients. Here's a look at some of the highlights from 2024 and how they're shaping the future of local marketing.

Winning Local Audiences with NIL Endorsements

2024 showcased the power of NIL (Name, Image, Likeness) endorsements as a game-changing tool for local businesses, and Watchmaker Marketing was at the forefront. One of our standout campaigns featured a collaboration with Epic Seats and Michael Penix Jr. during his Heisman-nominated season, which culminated in a thrilling run to the National Championship in January 2024. By connecting Epic Seats with Penix Jr.'s loyal and engaged fanbase, we helped the brand grow its visibility and build meaningful awareness; particularly with diehard Huskies in Epic Seat's hometown, Seattle.

Streaming TV Advertising: Local Brands on the Big Screen

Streaming TV remained one of 2024's most impactful advertising channels, and we helped our clients harness its full potential. Platforms like Paramount+, Peacock, and the newly expanded Prime Video offering from Amazon provided an unmatched combination of premium content, advanced targeting, and in-depth reporting. These tools allowed us to craft highly tailored campaigns for local businesses—even those in quieter corners of Washington, such as Bayside Coin & Jewelry in Bellingham and West Hills Auto Plex in Bremerton. Through high-profile programming like Yellowstone (Paramount+), Rings of Power (Amazon), and NFL games on Peacock, we made it possible for these businesses to reach larger, more engaged audiences than ever before.

Harnessing AI to Transform Local Advertising

At Watchmaker Marketing, 2024 marked a breakthrough year in our use of artificial intelligence to elevate creative campaigns. We leveraged advanced AI platforms to design dynamic animated ads for Hunt's Services, bringing their messaging to life in visually stunning ways. Beyond animation, we used AI to craft detailed and engaging storyboards, enabling clients to visualize their campaigns with precision before production even began. For Watchmaker, AI isn't about replacing creativity; it's about amplifying it to unlock new possibilities for our clients.

Watch out for our next blog, where we'll explore the evolution of ad tech in 2025 and share how Watchmaker plans to incorporate emerging tools to keep our clients ahead of the curve.
